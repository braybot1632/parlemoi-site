ParleMoi is a mobile application designed to help users learn and practice the French language. The Service includes, but is not limited to:

  • Structured lessons covering vocabulary, grammar, reading, and listening comprehension.
  • Interactive exercises including quizzes, fill-in-the-blank activities, and matching games.

  • Pronunciation scoring that evaluates your spoken French and provides feedback.

Important Disclaimers About AI Features

Henri and Marie are AI-powered language practice tools. They are not certified language instructors, qualified teachers, or human tutors. Their responses are generated by artificial intelligence and are intended to simulate conversational practice in French. They do not hold teaching credentials and should not be treated as authoritative sources on French language rules, culture, or usage.

Pronunciation scoring is approximate. The pronunciation evaluation feature uses speech recognition technology to provide general feedback on your spoken French. Scores and suggestions are estimates and may not reflect the assessment a qualified linguist or language instructor would provide. Factors such as background noise, microphone quality, accent variation, and device hardware can affect accuracy. Pronunciation scores should be used as a general guide, not as a definitive measure of your speaking ability.

ParleMoi is a supplementary learning tool. It is not a replacement for formal language education, immersion programs, or instruction from certified professionals.
