Thank you for signing up to test ParleMoi. We’re excited to have you on board. Your TestFlight invitation is on the way — here’s everything you need to get started.

Getting set up

1 Check your email — you’ll receive a separate invitation from Apple TestFlight shortly. It may take a few minutes to arrive.
2 Install TestFlight — if you don’t already have it, download the TestFlight app from the App Store (it’s free, made by Apple).
3 Accept the invite — open the TestFlight email and tap "View in TestFlight". This will open the app and let you install ParleMoi.
4 Start learning — open ParleMoi, complete the onboarding, and explore at your own pace. There’s no right or wrong way to test — just use it naturally.

After you’ve tested the app

Once you’ve had a chance to explore, we’d love to hear what you think. Your feedback directly shapes what we build next, and the survey is completely anonymous — no user information is attached — so please be as candid as possible.

Take the Feedback Survey

The survey takes about 3 minutes and covers your experience with onboarding, lessons, AI chat, practice scenarios, and mini-games.
